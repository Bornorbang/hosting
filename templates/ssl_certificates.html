SSL Certificate FAQ

Common questions about SSL certificates

What is an SSL certificate?

An SSL certificate is a digital certificate that authenticates a website's identity and enables encrypted connections. It secures data transmitted between your website and visitors' browsers.

Why do I need SSL for my website?

SSL certificates protect sensitive data, build customer trust, improve SEO rankings, and are required for collecting payments online. Modern browsers also warn users about non-HTTPS sites.

What's the difference between DV, OV, and EV certificates?

Domain Validated (DV) certificates verify domain ownership only. Organization Validated (OV) certificates verify the organization. Extended Validation (EV) certificates provide the highest level of verification and display the company name in browsers.

Do you install SSL certificates for free?

Yes! We provide free SSL certificate installation and configuration for all our customers. Our technical team handles the entire setup process to ensure proper implementation.

How long does it take to get an SSL certificate?

Domain Validated certificates are issued within 5 minutes after domain verification. Extended Validation certificates may take 1-3 business days due to additional verification requirements.
