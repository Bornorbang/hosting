WHOIS Lookup FAQ

Common questions about WHOIS domain lookups

What is WHOIS?

WHOIS is a protocol used to query databases that store information about registered domain names and IP addresses. It provides details about domain ownership, registration dates, and contact information.

Is WHOIS information always accurate?

WHOIS information is provided by domain registrants and is generally accurate, but it can sometimes be outdated or protected by privacy services. Registrars are required to verify contact information periodically.

Can I lookup any domain?

You can lookup most domain names and IP addresses. However, some domains may have privacy protection enabled, which hides the registrant's personal information and shows privacy service details instead.

How often is WHOIS data updated?

WHOIS data is updated whenever domain information changes, such as when ownership transfers, contact details are modified, or name servers are updated. Updates typically appear within 24-48 hours.
