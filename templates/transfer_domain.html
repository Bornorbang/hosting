Transfer FAQ

Common questions about domain transfers

How long does a domain transfer take?

Domain transfers typically take 5-7 days to complete. During this time, your website and email will continue working normally. You'll receive updates throughout the process.

Will my website go down during transfer?

No, your website and email services will continue working normally throughout the transfer process. There is absolutely no downtime when transferring domains.

What is an authorization code (EPP code)?

An authorization code is a unique password for your domain that proves you own it. You can get this from your current registrar's control panel or by contacting their support.

Can I transfer a domain that expires soon?

Yes, but the domain must have at least 60 days remaining before expiration to be eligible for transfer. We recommend transferring well before expiration for the smoothest process.

What happens to my current hosting?

Domain transfer only moves domain registration, not hosting. Your website hosting stays with your current provider unless you also choose to transfer hosting to us.
