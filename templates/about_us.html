Our Story

Founded in Lagos in 2018, Hosting Nigeria was born from a simple observation: Nigerian businesses needed reliable, affordable web hosting solutions designed specifically for the local market.

Our founder, Adebayo Ogundimu, was frustrated by the lack of quality hosting options that understood the unique challenges of doing business in Nigeria. After years of working with international providers that offered poor local support and didn't understand Nigerian payment systems, he decided to create a better solution.

Today, we're proud to serve over 5,000 Nigerian businesses, from small startups in Abuja to large enterprises in Port Harcourt. Our locally-based team provides round-the-clock support in English, Yoruba, Hausa, and Igbo.
