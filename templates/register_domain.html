Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about domain registration and management

What is a domain name?

A domain name is your website's address on the internet (like hostingnigeria.com). It's what people type into their browser to visit your website. Think of it as your digital address that makes your website accessible to everyone online.

Which domain extension should I choose?

For Nigerian businesses, we recommend .com.ng or .ng for local credibility, or .com for global reach. .com is the most recognized worldwide, while .ng shows you're a Nigerian business. Choose based on your target audience and business goals.

Can I transfer my existing domain to you?

Yes! We offer free domain transfers with no downtime. The process typically takes 5-7 days and we'll guide you through every step. You'll also get an additional year added to your domain registration when you transfer.

Do I need hosting to register a domain?

No, you can register a domain without hosting. However, to have a working website, you'll need both a domain and hosting. We offer discounted hosting when you register a domain with us, making it convenient to get everything in one place.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major Nigerian payment methods including bank transfers, Paystack, Flutterwave, and major credit/debit cards. All payments are processed securely and you'll receive instant confirmation.

What happens if my domain expires?

We'll send multiple renewal reminders before expiration. After expiry, you have a 30-day grace period to renew at regular price, followed by a 30-day redemption period (with additional fees). Don't worry - we'll help you avoid any issues.
