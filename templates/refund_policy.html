Refund Policy

We stand behind our services with a customer-friendly refund policy. Learn about our money-back guarantee and refund procedures.

Our Commitment to Customer Satisfaction

At Hosting Nigeria, we are committed to providing exceptional web hosting services to Nigerian businesses. We understand that choosing a hosting provider is an important decision, which is why we offer a comprehensive refund policy to ensure your satisfaction and peace of mind.

This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are processed, helping you understand your rights and our procedures for handling refund requests.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee on new shared hosting accounts to ensure you're completely satisfied with our services.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Eligible Services

Our 30-day money-back guarantee applies to:

  • New shared hosting account purchases
  • New VPS hosting account purchases
  • WordPress hosting plans
  • Business hosting packages

Guarantee Terms

Timeframe

Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of your initial service purchase

Coverage

Covers hosting fees only; excludes domain registrations and add-on services

Processing Time

Refunds processed within 7-14 business days after approval

Payment Method

Refunded to the original payment method used for the purchase

Conditions for Eligibility

To qualify for our money-back guarantee, the following conditions must be met:

  • Account must be in good standing with no violations of our Terms of Service
  • No excessive resource usage that impacts other customers
  • No abuse of our support system or services
  • Account must not have been used for prohibited activities
  • Request must be made by the original account holder
  • All outstanding invoices must be current

Non-Refundable Items and Services

The following items and services are non-refundable under all circumstances:

Domain-Related Services

  • Domain name registrations (.com, .ng, .com.ng, etc.)
  • Domain renewals and transfers
  • Domain privacy protection services
  • DNS management fees

Security Services

  • SSL certificates (free and premium)
  • SiteLock security services
  • Malware removal services
  • Security audits and assessments

Setup and Professional Services

  • One-time setup fees
  • Migration services
  • Custom configuration services
  • Consultation and training fees

Third-Party Services

  • Third-party software licenses
  • External service integrations
  • Partner service fees
  • Marketplace purchases

Important Note: Domain registrations are processed through third-party registrars and cannot be refunded once registered. Domain names remain valid for their registration period.

Refund Exclusions

Refunds will not be processed in the following situations:

Policy Violations

  • Accounts suspended or terminated for Terms of Service violations
  • Acceptable Use Policy breaches
  • Fraudulent activities or payment disputes
  • Spam or malicious content hosting
  • Resource abuse or server overload

Service Usage

  • Services used beyond the 30-day guarantee period
  • Downgrade requests (use service credit instead)
  • Requests made by unauthorized parties
  • Partial billing period refunds (except for early termination)

Special Circumstances

  • Promotional or discounted pricing (may have different terms)
  • Custom enterprise agreements (governed by specific contract terms)
  • Bundled services (refund applies to eligible components only)
  • Reseller accounts (different refund terms may apply)

Partial Refunds and Service Credits

Pro-Rated Refunds

In certain situations, we may provide pro-rated refunds:

  • Early termination of annual plans (after 30-day guarantee period)
  • Service downgrades with significant billing differences
  • Service interruptions due to our technical issues (SLA credits)
  • Billing errors or duplicate charges

Service Credits

We may offer service credits instead of cash refunds for:

  • Service level agreement (SLA) violations
  • Downtime compensation
  • Plan downgrades within the guarantee period
  • Customer satisfaction issues

Service credits are applied to your account and can be used for future services. Credits do not expire and can be transferred if you change hosting plans.

How to Request a Refund

Step-by-Step Process

1

Submit a Request

Contact our support team through your account dashboard, email, or phone to initiate a refund request.

2

Provide Information

Include your account details, reason for the refund request, and any relevant documentation.

3

Account Review

Our billing team will review your account and eligibility for a refund within 2-3 business days.

4

Decision Notification

You'll receive an email notification with our decision and next steps.

5

Refund Processing

If approved, your refund will be processed within 7-14 business days to your original payment method.

Required Information

To expedite your refund request, please provide:

  • Your account username or customer ID
  • Email address associated with the account
  • Invoice number or transaction ID
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Preferred refund method (if different from original payment)

Special Circumstances and Exceptions

Promotional Offers

Services purchased at promotional prices may have different refund terms:

  • Limited-time offers may have shorter refund periods
  • Bundle deals may require returning all components
  • Discount coupons may affect refund amounts
  • Free trial extensions may modify guarantee periods

Payment Method Restrictions

Refund processing may vary by payment method:

  • Credit card refunds: 7-14 business days
  • Bank transfer refunds: 5-10 business days
  • Digital wallet refunds: 3-7 business days
  • Cash payments: Account credit or bank transfer

International Customers

For customers outside Nigeria, additional considerations may apply including currency conversion, international transfer fees, and local banking regulations. Refund processing times may be extended for international transactions.

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision, we offer the following resolution options:

Internal Review

  • Request a second review by our senior billing team
  • Provide additional documentation or evidence
  • Escalate to management for complex cases

External Options

  • Contact the Nigerian Consumer Protection Council
  • File a complaint with relevant regulatory bodies
  • Pursue mediation through recognized Nigerian institutions
